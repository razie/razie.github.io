K1 (11-12)
Nancy Greene League Racing is a team concept program for girls and boys 7 to 10
years old. At this level we stress the FUNdamentals with and emphasis on FUN!
The season runs eight consecutive weeks on Saturday. There is also additional,
optional Sunday training available for these racers (additional fee applies). Within
these eight weeks are four races and a fun race with the remaining days set aside for
training.
K1 League Racing builds on team effort and points but also rewards individual
achievements with podium recognition. Girls and boys ages 11 and 12 are eligible for
the K1 level. At this level more development of technique and ability come into
play…without forgetting the Fun aspect.
The season runs for eight consecutive weeks on Saturdays and Sundays with mixing
together training, race days, semi finals and finals.
In addition, Alpine offers a number of supplementary weekday programs for K1 racers.
The season starts with a Christmas Camp (highly recommended) when new racers learn the
racing techniques and the returning racers get a chance to build on last seasons successes.
Training days start at 9:30am and consist of four hours of coaching with a one hour lunch
break. Video taping and reviews provide athletes and coaches with a great learning tool.
