Glacier Ski Club is an incorporated not for profit ski club with approximately 100
racers. We are a competitive racing club but our motto is "to have fun while helping
each other to achieve our best". We are family-oriented and rely on a strong
network of volunteers to ensure a well-coordinated and successful club.


Our competitive ski program, delivered by C.S.C.F. level trained coaches,
prepares children and teenagers to compete in the Alpine Ontario ski racing
league in Southern Ontario (slalom and GS), within the following four age groups -

- Nancy Greene League Racing (ages 7 to 10)
- K1 League Racing (ages 11 and 12),
- K2 League Racing (ages 13 and 14) and
- J Alpine League Racing (ages 15 to 18)


Glacier operates out of the Mt St Loius Moonstone ski area, just north of Barrie.
League races take place at various ski clubs and resorts in and around southern
Ontario.
